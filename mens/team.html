Dr. Harji Kaur

Dermatologist • Cosmetologist • Venereologist
  • Experience: 21+ Years
  • Education: MBBS – JNMC, AMU
  • Specialisation: DNB Dermatology
  • International Training: Dip GUM (UK)

Dr. Harji Kaur is a senior dermatologist with over 21 years of experience in clinical dermatology, cosmetology, and venereology.

She practices at Safdarjung Enclave, Delhi and has completed her MBBS from Jawahar Lal Nehru Medical College (AMU), followed by DNB in Dermatology & Venereology from the National Board of Examinations, Government of India.

Her expertise includes skin rejuvenation, anti-ageing treatments, pigmentation correction, chemical peels, microdermabrasion and complete skin care for all skin types. All treatments are ethical, personalised, and result-driven.
